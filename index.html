My life, where each week I've been alive is a little box. When meaningful things happen (for both better and worse) I make a note of it so I can remember how much of life is influenced by these factors. If you'd like to create your own version of this, it's basically a data file, a template, and a homepage mashed together on Jekyll blog hosted on Netlify.

This is a specialized page from my main site brennan.day, focused specifically on the life in weeks visualization.

Inspired by Buster Benson's Life in Weeks and Tim Urban's "Your Life in Weeks".
